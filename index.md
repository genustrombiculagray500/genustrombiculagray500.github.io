### Time-Based Effects
The bundle includes delay and echo units. These plugins generate complex rhythmic patterns and spatial width. Use them for lead vocals, electric guitars, or synthesizers to add interest to your soundstage.

### Modulation Tools
Modulation effects shift pitch, phase, or frequency over time. These plugins add movement to static recordings. You can create swooshing effects or broaden the stereo image of your tracks.
